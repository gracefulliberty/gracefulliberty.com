Hello! I'm Anna. I'm a physics and computer science student with a degree in Physics and Astronomy.

I have about 10 years of non-professional experience writing and maintaining software and as a systems administrator. I prefer to work building fundamental libraries for bigger projects, but I'm also proficient with Web technologies and UI Design. My favorite language to work with is Rust, but I am also proficient in C, Java, Go, Python, JavaScript and several others.

I'm passionate about making software that's understandable for people through visual, literate, and natural language programming. To that end, I'm working on a tool called [ReTangled](https://codeberg.org/liberty/retangled) to make literate programming more accessible. I write about my thoughts related to this and other topics in my [articles](/articles).

Follow me on [Mastodon](https://mathstodon.xyz/@liberty) for regular updates on what I'm doing.
